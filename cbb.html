Your loan withdrawal transfer request from your profile account to your linked bank account requires final clearance by the Central Bank of Barbados (CBB).

Under the Foreign Exchange Control Act, all outbound transfers must be validated with a registered CBB Foreign Exchange Authorization Code.

This ensures that the transaction complies with both national banking regulations and international fund transfer standards.
